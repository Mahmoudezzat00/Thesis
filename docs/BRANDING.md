# Brand Identity

## Name

SmartShop Thesis

## Positioning

SmartShop Thesis is positioned as an analytics-focused ecommerce system. The brand should feel more like a decision-support platform than a generic online shop.

## Visual Direction

- **Primary color:** blue, used for trust, clarity, and data-driven interaction.
- **Secondary accent:** teal, used in the logo to suggest growth and analytics.
- **Logo concept:** a shopping bag combined with an upward analytics line.
- **Tone:** practical, academic, and product-focused.

## Why This Matters

Because the project began from an existing ecommerce codebase, the visual identity helps distinguish the thesis implementation. The logo, name, color palette, documentation, and intelligence features all support the same thesis direction: ecommerce plus explainable commerce analytics.
